About Us.

The Rotaract Club is a global organization of young adults who are dedicated to leadership, professional development, and community service. Operating under the guidance of Rotary International, Rotaract CINEC clubs empower members to develop leadership skills, build lasting friendships, and create positive change in their communities. Through various projects and initiatives, Rotaract members address pressing social issues, promote peace, and foster goodwill worldwide.
